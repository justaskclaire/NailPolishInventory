This Privacy Policy describes:

By using our Website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using our Website:

  • Browsing Preferences: Polish favorites, next appointment selections, and filter preferences stored locally in your browser
  • Contact Information: If you contact us directly (name, email address, message content)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our Website, we may automatically collect certain technical information:

Our Website integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Pinterest API: If you use our inspiration browsing feature, we access Pinterest's public API to display nail design images. We do not collect your Pinterest login credentials. Any Pinterest data accessed is subject to Pinterest's Privacy Policy.

Important: We do NOT store, sell, or share any information obtained through the Pinterest API with third parties. We only use it to display inspiration images and suggest polish colors to you in real-time.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide and maintain our Website functionality (browsing polishes, saving favorites)
  • Personalization: To remember your preferences (favorites, next appointment selections) using local browser storage
  • Inspiration Matching: To analyze Pinterest images and suggest polish colors from our collection
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Improvement: To understand how our Website is used and improve its features
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Your personal preferences (favorite polishes, next appointment selections) are stored locally in your browser using browser local storage technology. This data:

  • Remains on YOUR device only
  • Is NOT transmitted to our servers
  • Is NOT accessible to other users
  • Can be cleared by you at any time through your browser settings

4.2 Pinterest API Data

In accordance with Pinterest's Developer Terms and our API agreement:

  • We do NOT store any Pinterest user data, pins, or board information on our servers
  • We fetch Pinterest data in real-time when you use the inspiration feature
  • Pinterest images are displayed directly from Pinterest's content delivery network
  • All Pinterest data remains subject to Pinterest's control and policies

4.3 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS encryption for all website communications
  • Secure handling of any contact form submissions
  • Regular security assessments and updates

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services that help us operate our Website (e.g., web hosting providers), under strict confidentiality agreements

  • Protection of Rights: To protect our rights, property, safety, or the rights of others
